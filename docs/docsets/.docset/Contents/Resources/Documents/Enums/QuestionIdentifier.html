QuestionIdentifier

public enum QuestionIdentifier: String

Identifier used to identify questions and the matching results.

  • LosingInterest: Having little interest or pleasure.
  • FeelingDepressed: Feeling depressed.
  • TroubleSleeping: Trouble sleeping or sleeping too much.
  • FeelingTired: Feeling tired or having little energy.
  • PoorAppetite: Poor appetite or overeating.
  • LowSelfEsteem: Feeling bad about yourself or like a failure.
  • TroubleConcentrating: Trouble concentrating on things.
  • SlowOrFast: Moving or speaking slowly or more than usual.
  • FeelingSuicidal: Thoughts to be better off dead or of hurting yourself.
